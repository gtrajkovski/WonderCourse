# v1.0 Roadmap Archive

**Milestone:** v1.0
**Completed:** 2026-02-12

## Summary

Course Builder Studio v1.0 delivers an AI-powered course authoring platform that generates complete Coursera short courses from high-level blueprints. The release includes:

- **13 phases** executed across **87 plans**
- **90 requirements** satisfied
- **11 content generators** (video scripts, readings, quizzes, HOL, coach, labs, discussions, assignments, projects, rubrics, textbooks)
- **User authentication** with session management
- **Collaboration** with role-based permissions
- **Full UI** with dark theme
- **AI interactive features** (inline editing, coach chat, content import)
- **Export** to instructor packages, LMS manifests, SCORM, and DOCX

## Key Accomplishments

1. **Complete Content Generation Suite** — 11 generators covering all Coursera activity types with WWHAA structure, Bloom's alignment, and quality validation
2. **AI-Powered Blueprint Generation** — Users describe course goals and AI generates full module/lesson/activity hierarchy
3. **Interactive AI Coach** — Real-time Socratic tutoring with guardrails, evaluation, and transcript storage
4. **Multi-User Collaboration** — Role-based permissions (owner, designer, reviewer, SME) with commenting, audit trails, and activity feeds
5. **Export-Ready Packaging** — Instructor ZIP bundles, LMS manifests, SCORM packages, and DOCX textbooks
6. **End-to-End Polish** — Error handling, retry decorators, skeleton loading, onboarding tours, and E2E tests
